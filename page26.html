ROCuronium

Also known by it's brand name, Zemuron.

  1. What Is It - ROCuronium is an antagonist drug used for paralysis.
    Starts working within 10-15 minutes.
    Lasts 30-90 minutes. 
  2. How It Works - Unlike nondepolarizing drugs that bind onto a receptor and depolarizes it, drugs such as ROCuronium and VECuronium are antagonists and block receptors. This means that the normal neurotransmitter can't bind so you also get paralysis. 
  3. Why It's Unique - ROCuronium is a perfered drug for long term paralysis during surgery.  You can also give a high dose for rapid paralysis (90 seconds or so) for intubation.
