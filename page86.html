Toradol

Also known as, Ketorolac.

  1. What Is It - For the most part, Toradol is a cute name for ibuprofen, although they are different drugs, but pretty close to the same thing. 
  2. How It Works - Toradol is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID), and it works by blocking the enzymes cells used to make prostaglandins. As a result, pain and inflammation, and signs and symptoms of redness, swelling, fever, and pain, are reduced.
  3. Why It's Unique - Toradol is more effective than ibuprofen.
