Famotidine

Also known as, Pepcid.

  1. What Is It - Pepcid is an antihistamine. Helps with nausea, vomiting, and sedation. 
  2. How It Works - It works by blocking histamines that your body makes during an alergic reaction. it's dying effects on such symptoms as watery eyes, and runny nose are caused by blocking another natural substance made by your body (acetylcholine). 
  3. Why It's Unique - Benadryl is a histamine antagonist, so it targets histamine receptor H-1 to have it's effects. Similarly, Pepcid is an H-2 blocker, so if you have a true allergic reaction, you should give both to cover all your bases.
