EIMS

Easy to use, easy to forget how to use.

Step 1

Double click the patient.

Step 2

Click on 'Routing Ticket' and then assign the anesthesiologist.

Step 3

Click on 'Impressions', and then click on 'MCF Intra-Op TEE', and finally, click on the three dots and then 'Stage' from the menu that drops from those three dots.

Step 4

 Scroll to the bottom of the “Staging” window, and click Save.

Step 5

After you click save, there will be several errors. Select the red box under 'Surgery' and click the blue box with a + and put a 1 in the free text space as seen in the picture above.

Step 6

Then do the same as step 5 under 'Post-Bypass left ventricular ejection fraction', and in the free text box enter: 00

Step 7
The scroll to the box under that (Post-bypass) and select “normal”.



Step 8
Click Save. You're done!
