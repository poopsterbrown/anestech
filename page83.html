Metaprolol

Only known as, Lopressor.

  1. What Is It - A beta blocker, which means it reduces blood pressure.

  2. How It Works - Metoprolol is selective for only beta 1 blocking. 

  3. Why It's Unique - It blood pressure lowering effect is greater in metaprolol than it is in labetalol. Labetalol gets used less for asthmatics or severe COPD as by blocking beta 2, you risk causing slight bronchoconstriction (as beta 2 normally bronchodilates so by blocking you have the opposite effect).
